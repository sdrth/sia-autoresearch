# Bets

## Bet 1: Segment existing users by industry before choosing anything

**Claim:** The beachhead vertical is already visible in current usage data — activated accounts concentrate in 1–2 industries the team has never measured.

**Build / test next:** Tag the last 200 signups by industry (enrichment + manual pass). Falsified if activated accounts are evenly spread with no vertical above ~20%.

## Bet 2: Vertical template pack as a demand and activation test

**Claim:** A template pack for the top vertical from Bet 1 will outperform generic templates on first-session activation, because it fixes the blank state (run 001) with industry-native artifacts.

**Build / test next:** Ship 5 templates + one vertical landing page for the top vertical. Compare first-value-event rate against the generic-template cohort over 30 days.

## Bet 3: Ten pull interviews in the beachhead vertical

**Claim:** Interviews with current users in the top vertical will surface 2–3 industry-specific workflow gaps that justify (or kill) deeper verticalization — before any product fork.

**Build / test next:** Interview 10 activated users from the Bet 1 vertical. Killed if no workflow gap is mentioned by at least 3 of 10 independently.
